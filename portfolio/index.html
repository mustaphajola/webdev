What I am working on

Data Assimilation

Data assimilation is the technique of using observed data to update model. The existing model is updated by adjusting its initial paramters to fit recently observed data. Data assimialtion is essentially an inverse problem and mostly used in numerical weather predicition.

Generative Networks

Generative networks are a ML framework that are trained to mimic the statistical distribtuion of a specific dataset thus allowing us to generate "fake" samples that seem to belong to that distribution. Recent works in this field of ML has shown great success indicating the huge promise these kind of networks have.
